Sociocracy 3.0

…draws on collective wisdom and motivates
the full engagement of every member
to effectively steer the organization.

…enables the continuous evolution of
organisational structure according to
changing context.

Learn more ≫
 

…complements and enriches Scrum,
Kanban and the Lean Startup Method.

…is sociocracy (a.k.a. dynamic governance) updated for the 21st century
and integrated with lean and agile thinking.

How Does It Work?

Structure

circles

 Organize work in semi-autonomous, self-organizing and often self-governing teams.

scale

 A variety of structural patterns that can be combined to organically grow and evolve your organisation.

align

 Circles and individuals align towards shared vision, mission and values.
Learn how ≫
 
People

elect

Identify appropriate people for roles.

improve

Maximise potential of everyone in the organization, amplyfiy individual development and learning.
Information

integrate

 Use fast and effective processes to synthesize diverse viewpoints into workable solutions.

iterate

 Start with policies that are good enough for now and safe enough to try. Evolve your policies as new wisdom emerges.

link

 Connect circles for effective flow and processing of information throughout the organization.
